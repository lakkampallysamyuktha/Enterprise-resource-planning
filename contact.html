Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about deployment, support, and integration.

How long does the typical ERP deployment take?

Deployment timelines vary based on company size and custom requirements. For SMBs, a standard deployment usually takes 4-8 weeks. Enterprise deployments can span 3-6 months. We provide a tailored roadmap during the 'Plan' phase.

Is technical support available 24/7?

Yes, all Enterprise and Pro tier customers receive 24/7 dedicated support via phone, live chat, and a priority ticketing system. Standard tier customers have access to business-hours support and a comprehensive self-serve knowledge base.

Can Stackly integrate with my existing tools?

Absolutely. We offer over 500+ native integrations with popular software like Salesforce, Slack, AWS, and modern HR platforms. If you have custom in-house tools, our REST API allows your development team to easily connect them to Stackly.

Do you provide on-site training for my team?

We provide comprehensive digital onboarding for all tiers. On-site, in-person training is available as an add-on service for Enterprise customers, where our deployment specialists will visit your offices to ensure smooth adoption.
